About Us

HOTEL SRI PUNJABI'S DHABA is a family-owned restaurant specializing in traditional Punjabi cuisine. We are committed to providing authentic flavors and exceptional service to our customers.

Our journey began with a passion for bringing the rich, traditional flavors of Punjab to food lovers everywhere. Every dish we serve is prepared with carefully selected spices, fresh ingredients, and time-tested recipes passed down through generations.

We pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather to enjoy delicious food and make lasting memories. Our menu features a wide variety of dishes, from hearty biriyanis and tandoori specialties to vegetarian delights and comforting soups.

Thank you for choosing Hotel Sri Punjabi's Dhaba. We look forward to serving you!
